The Hog Token is a community focused DeFI token residing on the Binance Smart Chain. Full details of the inner workings is presented in our Whitepaper.

Token features

Each trade

  • 1% will go to a buyback wallet that will be used to burn tokens.
  • 2% will be reflected proportionally to token holders.
  • 4% will be automatically converted into liquidity.
  • 5% will be redirected to the marketing wallet in order to aid continued growth and stability.
